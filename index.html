About Me

Welcome to Suriosity.com, an informal website where I would like to tell you a little about myself.

From the picture and caption above, you'll see what I look like and my name. A picture can be worth a thousand words but I would like to give you a thousand and two words about myself, so here it is. I am a proud Panther, representing FIU; I am a motivated and responsible person, always trying to give 110%; I am a curious young man, trying to figure out what life is all about and how things work. My ambitions and desires in life are simple: I want to be happy most of all, and I want to become an acknowledged software developer.

My interest in computers has been around since I was a young child and has cultivated into a passion that I like to sink myself into whenever I can. When I was first introduced to programming, I was left stupefied. I was flabbergasted at what I could make a computer do; and at that point all I had done was make a computer say "Hello World." I enrolled in an "Intro to C++" class and learned about the basic terms and concepts in Computer Science. I was able to use loops and read files and do much more which astounded me at that time. But then I realized that the more I knew, the more I didn't know. When given the choice to continue the course and take the second level "Objected Oriented Programming with C++", I gladly took it. I was intellectually challenged with the once daunting concepts such as linked lists and inheritance. Fortunately for me, I had a friend, who become a mentor in many ways, and another pal, who I was taking the class with, help explain, conceptualize, and soldify the concepts.

Since then, I've dabbled with HTML/CSS and PHP (which I used to create this website), became familiar with the Linux shell, and am now in pursuit of understanding another object oriented programming language: Java. In the coming future, I know I would like to learn about Mobile App Development, Virtual Reality and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning.

Besides that, in my free time, I like to enjoy the natural beauty of life. Living in Miami has given me no shortage of the ability to do as such. I love going to the beach and seeing the never-ending horizon atop the water and the awe-inspiring sunsets. I also love to play and watch basketball. My favorite team in the NBA is, and always has been the Miami Heat. Besides basketball, my friends have also built up my involvement and liking of ultimate frisbee.
